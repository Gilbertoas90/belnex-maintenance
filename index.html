      About Belnex

      FAQ

      Frequently asked questions

      Do you work with both residential and commercial clients?

      Yes. We design and install for homeowners and businesses across Belgium, from a single EV charger to full commercial solar and battery systems.

      How long does an installation take?

      Most residential projects (electrical, solar, EV charger) are completed in 1–3 days on site. Larger commercial jobs are scoped and scheduled individually after a site visit.

      Are your installations certified?

      Yes. All work is inspected and certified against Belgian electrical code, and our team holds RESCert and VCA safety certifications.

      Can I combine solar, batteries and an EV charger in one project?

      That is exactly the kind of integrated project we specialize in — one team, one design, no coordination gaps between separate contractors.

      Do you offer financing?

      We can point you to financing partners as part of your quote — ask your Belnex advisor about current options for your project.

      What areas in Belgium do you serve?

      We currently serve homeowners and businesses across Belgium. Get in touch with your location and we will confirm coverage for your project.
